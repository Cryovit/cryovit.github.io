QoL

Pre-Infinity

Unlike NG-5, you do not start with any direct nerf at this stage. However, you will still be slowed by achievements gradually.

Autobuyer

Autobuyers cannot be unlocked before infinity. You have to finish corresponding Normal Challenge to unlock them, and corresponding Automation Challenge to upgrade them.

Infinity

Normal Challenge

All normal challenges now only unlocks the autobuyer, not unlocking their upgrades.

Unlock: only C1 is available after an eternity or reality. You have to pay IP to unlock other challenges.

Automation Challenge

These challenges unlock the upgrade of corresponding normal autobuyers. For example, AC1 unlocks the upgrade of autobuyer unlocked by C1, which is 1st AD autobuyer.

Eternity Upgrade

EU31: Reduce post-infinity Infinity Dimension cost multiplier scaling: In masochist mod, infinity dimension cost scales super-exponentially after 1.8e308 IP. The cost multiplier is multiplied by 10 per purchase, just like antimatter dimension cost scales above 1.8e308 AM.

EU31 lowers the scale. Unlike AD, this scale can be lowered to x1, disabling the super-exponential growth.

  • The first 24 tiers of EU31 decreases the expotent of ID cost scaling (abbreviated as IDSE). Each tier lowers IDSE by 1/25, making ID cost scaling decreased from 10 to 10^(1/25).
  • The 25th to 29th tiers further divide IDSE by 10 each, making ID cost scaling decreased from 10^(1/25) to 10^(1/2500000).
  • The 30th tier, finally, set IDSE to 0, so ID cost scaling is disabled.

The cost of this upgrade scales super-exponentially, or more specifically, the exponent of the cost grows cubically.

  • The base cost is 10 EP, and the base price multiplier is 10.
  • The price multiplier is multiplied by 10 per purchase, with this 10 further multiplied by 10 per purchase.
  • The 30th tier costs 1e4090 EP, which is beyond the minimal requirement for Reality.

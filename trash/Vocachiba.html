What is VocaChiba?

VocaChiba is a monthly DJ event held in Chiba, Japan.

On the first Sunday of every month, three Vocaloid producers will DJ dozens of Vocaloid songs, remixing and mashing them together, releasing new songs, and sharing their love for Vocaloid music.

As of today, VocaChiba is also available as TuneCore albums, providing a new way for Vocaloid fans to enjoy the music. (Albums, both physical and digital, are recorded before the live event, and may sound different from the live event.)

Standard Banner

Base Prob.

  • UR: 2.0%
  • SSR: 8.0%
  • SR: 50.0%
  • R: 40.0%

How does it work?

Every 10th pull is guaranteed to be at least an SR. If you pull an SR or above before the 10th pull, the counter resets. (This is done by simply rerolling the RNG until it hits an SR or above, replacing the original result.)

After 20 consecutive pulls without an SSR, the probability distribution changes to:

  • UR: 2.0%
  • SSR: (8 + 4 * (n - 20))% when n<=30, (48 + 2.5 * (n - 30))% when n>30
  • SR: 50.0% when n<=30, (50 - 2.5 * (n - 30))% when n>30
  • R: (40 - 2 * (n - 20))% when n<=30, 0% when n>30

where n is the number of consecutive pulls without an SSR.

Thus every 50th pull is guaranteed to be at least an SSR. If you pull an SSR or above before the 50th pull, the counter resets.

After 50 consecutive pulls without a UR, the probability distribution changes to:

  • UR: (2 + 2 * (n - 50))%

The distribution of SSR, SR, and R changes depending on both the SSR and UR guarantee counters:

Each pull beyond 50th pull without a UR increases the probability of getting a UR by 2%, by reducing the probability of getting an R by 2%. If R probability reaches 0%, reduce the probability of getting an SR by 2% instead. If SR probability reaches 0%, reduce the probability of getting an SSR by 2% instead.

Thus every 99th pull is guaranteed to be a UR. If you pull a UR before the 99th pull, the counter resets.

Limited Banner

Base Prob.

  • UR: 4.0%
  • SSR: 16.0%
  • SR: 50.0%
  • R: 30.0%

How does it work?

Every 10th pull is guaranteed to be at least an SR. If you pull an SR or above before the 10th pull, the counter resets.

After 20 consecutive pulls without an SSR, the probability distribution changes to:

  • UR: 4.0%
  • SSR: (16 + 3 * (n - 20))% when n<=30, (46 + 2.5 * (n - 30))% when n>30
  • SR: 50.0% when n<=30, (50 - 2.5 * (n - 30))% when n>30
  • R: (30 - 3 * (n - 20))% when n<=30, 0% when n>30

where n is the number of consecutive pulls without an SSR.

Thus every 50th pull is guaranteed to be at least an SSR. If you pull an SSR or above before the 50th pull, the counter resets.

After 50 consecutive pulls without a UR, the probability distribution changes to:

  • UR: (4 + 4 * (n - 50))%

The mechanism is basically the same as the standard banner, except the base probability and probability increment are doubled.

Thus every 74th pull is guaranteed to be a UR. If you pull a UR before the 74th pull, the counter resets.

SSR Guaranteed Ticket

Base Prob.

  • UR: 10.0%
  • SSR: 90.0%

How does it work?

Every consecutive pull without an UR increases the probability of getting an UR by 10%, by reducing the probability of getting an SSR by 10%.

Unless you pull an UR before the 10th pull, the probability of getting an UR will reach 100% on the 10th pull. Thus it is guaranteed to get an UR within 10 pulls.

By pulling 100 times in a Standard Banner, or 150 times in a Limited Banner, you will get 1 SSR Guaranteed Ticket. The counter keeps between different Standard/Limited Banners, but does not share between the two types of banners, nor counts special banners (including Beginner’s Banner, SSR Guaranteed Ticket Banner, and crossover banners).

You can also purchase 10 SSR Guaranteed Tickets as part of the paid SSR Guaranteed Pack, which is available for purchase twice per year (during Anniversary in May and Thanksgiving in November), plus once for new players (within 30 days of account creation).

Monthly Subscription

You can purchase Monthly Subscription at any time, which is valid for 30 days (expires on 04:00 UTC+8 on the 31st day, given that purchase day is 1st day). You can purchase the subscription multiple times, but instead of the benefits stacking, the duration will be extended by 30 days, up to 90 days.

  • 550 Crystals / US$4.99 per 30 days
  • 30 Crystals per day for 30 days (=1,650 Crystals total) (C/$=291)
  • 10 Fuel per day for 30 days (~600 Crystals) -> 1.2x Battle Pass EXP for 30 days

3th Anniversary Update: Fuel is no longer included in the subscription. Instead, you gain 1.5x Battle Pass EXP for 30 days. The total value of the subscription shrinks, but is still very good. Also, this makes it easier for casual players to reach max level (100) within the season.

The total experience required and free (Std area) rewards both increases with this update, but the paid (Plus, Pro, Max area) rewards remain the same. Thus, the value of the subscription is reduced, but is still very good.

4th Anniversary Update: the Battle Pass EXP boost is reduced to 1.2x. Oh, no… But still, the base value of the subscription is already outstanding, and just regard the EXP boost as a slight bonus.

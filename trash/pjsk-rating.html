Rating consists of 2 parts: Base Rating and Match Rating.

Base Rating

Rating for each chart is calculated based on the following formula:

R = (5 + LV) * ACC ^ 4

where R is the rating, LV is the level of the chart, and ACC is the accuracy in percentage.

Different judgment types have different ACC values:

  • Perfect: 1.0
  • Great: 0.6
  • Good: 0.2
  • Bad: 0.0
  • Miss: 0.0

The final ACC is weighted average of all notes, where the weight of each note is the same as the weight in calculating score.

  • Tap: 1
  • Critical Tap: 2 (2x)
  • Slide (Head and Tail): 1
  • Critical Slide (Head and Tail): 2 (2x)
  • Slide (Body): 0.1
  • Critical Slide (Body): 0.1 (1x)
  • Slide (Body Special): 0.1
  • Critical Slide (Body Special): 0.2 (2x)
  • Flick: 2
  • Critical Flick: 6 (3x)

All chart rating is rounded down to 2 decimal places.

The total base rating is the sum of best 30 chart ratings, rounded down to nearest integer.

As of current version, theoretically the maximum base rating is 850.

Match Rating

Match rating is determined by match rank.

There are 7 ranks in total: Rookie, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, and Master.

Match score is calculated based on all notes judgement in the match, regardless of the note type:

  • Perfect: +1
  • Great: 0
  • Good: -2
  • Bad: -10
  • Miss: -20

Additionally, there are some bonus points:

  • Combo Bonus: +(max combo / 100)
  • All Perfect: +30
  • Full Combo: +10
  • Fail: -100
  • Facing a higher rank opponent: +10

You gain +1 match XP for winning, 0 for draw, and -1 for losing.

You don’t lose match XP in Rookie rank.

You advance to next rank when you reach certain amount of match XP:

  • Rookie: 3
  • Bronze: 6
  • Silver: 9
  • Gold: 12
  • Platinum: 15
  • Diamond: 20

… and start from 1 match XP in the next rank.

You demote to previous rank when you reach -2 match XP, and start from 2 match XP in the previous rank.

Match rating is determined by your current rank:

  • Rookie: 0
  • Bronze: 10
  • Silver: 30
  • Gold: 60
  • Platinum: 100
  • Diamond: 150
  • Master: 200 + match XP / 10, rounded down

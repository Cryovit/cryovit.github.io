VNMaker (Visual Novel Maker), as its name suggests, is a tool specialized in creating visual novels.

It is developed by Degica and published by Sekai Project.

Features

  • Modern, easy to use UI - VNMaker’s UI is modern and intuitive. It makes creating visual novels with few or no lines of code possible;
  • Highly customizable - VNMaker is highly customizable. Features including customizing UI, adding custom scripts, and more makes VNMaker a powerful tool, even for making RPGs;
  • Modular - VNMaker is modular. The base engine is free and lightweight, and you can purchase, share or create your own assets, scripts, plugins and more;
  • Asset management and co-creation - VNMaker has a built-in asset management system, which allows you to easily manage your assets. You can also share your assets with others, or even sell them on the Degica Store;
  • Multi-platform - VNMaker can export your visual novels to Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, iOS, and HTML5, playable on almost any device; (VNMaker itself is only available on Windows)
  • Internationalization and localization support - VNMaker supports multiple languages, and you can easily translate your visual novels to other languages with both AI tools and community help;
  • Built-in AI tools - AI scripting, drawing and voice acting tools are built-in, which lowers the barrier of creating visual novels; (Games with AI generated assets must be marked as such and restrictions on selling them may apply)
  • Efficient - Not only VNMaker is efficient and lightweight, but also the visual novels created with VNMaker are optimized for performance;
  • Standardized Scripting - VNMaker uses a standardized scripting language, Ink.js, which is easy to learn and use, but also highly extensible.

Pricing

VNMaker itself is free to use, but you need to purchase a license to publish your visual novels. The license is a one-time purchase, and you can publish as many visual novels as you want with it.

Some of the official assets and plugins are free, but most of them are paid. You can also purchase assets and plugins created by other users on the Degica Store.

Bundles are also available, which may save you some money. (Bundle may include some assets, plugins, or both. Every bundle also comes with a pricer version that includes VNMaker license. Bundles are time-limited, and different bundles are available at different times.)

  • VNMaker - Free (does not include license to publish visual novels)
  • Educational License - Free (for educational purposes only)
  • Individual License - $59.99/User (for sale value under $5000 and no more than 5 team members)
  • Team License - $99.99/User (for sale value under $50000 and no more than 20 team members)
  • Enterprise License - $199.99/User (for sale value under $500000 and no more than 100 team members)
  • Custom License - Contact Degica (for sale value over $500000 or more than 100 team members)
  • Startup Bundle - $49.99 (10 assets and 5 plugins) / $94.99 (Plus Individual License)
